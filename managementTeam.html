BJAS Murthy

Bjas Murthy is an intrinsically passionate and self-driven Business leader with over 2 decades of rich and prolific experience in the realm of Telecom and FMCG domain. A high impactful change-agent and versatile business growth leader with a very strong portfolio of successes, aggressively moving visions from concept to profitable reality with unequalled triumphs. Bjas held significant leadership roles with Vodafone India as Chief Operating Officer of one of its largest operations in India, to being the Country Head of Products & Services. He later moved on to successful roles of Operations Director of East and later North Operations before finally Heading the New Businesses and Partnerships for Vodafone India as his last stint. Bjas later moved to Africa to be the Group Chief Commercial Officer with Globacom, one of the largest operator in Western Africa heading their business in Nigeria, Ghana and Benin Republic.
